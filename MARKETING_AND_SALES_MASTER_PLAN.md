## 2. Marketing Feasibility & SWOT

### Feasibility Study
- **Market Demand:** Critical. With the 2026 "Indiana/Kentucky Privacy Acts" in full effect, corporate legal teams are forcing employees off cloud-based transcription. 
- **User Psychology:** Professionals are suffering "Bot Fatigue." An invisible, local instrument that doesn't "join" the call is the only viable path forward for high-stakes meetings.

### SWOT Analysis
- **Strengths:** 95% Gross Margins; Subpoena-proof storage; 0ms Latency; Airplane-mode functionality.
- **Weaknesses:** Hardware-specific (requires M-series/NPUs for peak performance); No easy cloud-sync (by design).
- **Opportunities:** Strategic partnerships with Law Societies and Medical Boards; Expansion into "Sovereign Team" local-network syncing.
- **Threats:** OS-level integration by Apple/Microsoft; Battery optimization challenges for long sessions.
